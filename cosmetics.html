About

Cosmetics is a company with many years of experience in the production of detergents, personal hygiene and cosmetics based on its own technology of processed herbs and top raw material bases. Cosmetics owns several brands, such as: Clavis, 2M, I&K Fluffy Pets and Reumax, on which its own product range is based. They wanted to refresh their website and logo with new modern and clean style. New color palete is made with soft colors. New symbol is leaf beacuse they made their product on natural base. Website is built in webflow with eCommerce and CMS extensions so owner can easily upload new stuff.
